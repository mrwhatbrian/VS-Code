What We're All About

At Shiny Science, our mission is simple: to inspire kids to explore, experiment, and learn the wonders of science, all while having a blast! The show blends curiosity and creativity with real-world science principles, creating an engaging, hands-on learning experience. Our goal is to spark imaginations, promote STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) education, and show that science isn’t just for classrooms, it’s for everyone!

Why It Matters

We believe that every child has the potential to be a scientist, an inventor, or an explorer. By combining humor, colorful experiments, and exciting adventures, Shiny Science makes learning fun and encourages children to keep asking ‘Why?’ and ‘How?’ while exploring the world around them.

Fun Science Facts

Lightning Fact

Did you know lightning can be five times hotter than the surface of the sun? Zap-tastic!

Ocean Trivia

The ocean is so deep that Mount Everest could fit inside it—and still be covered in water!

Colorful Chemistry

Why are flamingos pink? It’s because of the shrimp they eat!

Astronomy Fun

A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus! Now that’s a slow spin!

Animal Wonders

An octopus has three hearts! Two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.

Physics Wow

Did you know water can boil and freeze at the same time? It’s called the “triple point” and happens under special conditions!

Body Trivia

Your body has enough iron in it to make a small nail. That’s one powerful body!

Space Oddity

If you could fly to the sun in a regular airplane, it would take you about 19 years!

Cows Have Best Friends

They form strong bonds with certain herd mates and get stressed when separated.
